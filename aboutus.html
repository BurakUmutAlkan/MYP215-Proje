Duru Alkan

Interior Architecture / Architecture

Hello! I'm Duru, an interior architecture student with a passion for creating beautiful and functional spaces. My love for candles and interior design inspired me to create Noche, a brand dedicated to handcrafted candles that enhance any environment. Each candle is thoughtfully designed to bring warmth and style to your home.

At Noche, we believe that candles are more than just decorative items; they are essential elements that contribute to the ambiance and character of a space. Our candles are made with natural ingredients and infused with scents that evoke comfort and elegance. Join us on this journey to transform your living spaces with the perfect blend of design and fragrance.
