Noche Workshop

Join us at Noche Workshop to experience the art of candle making. Our hands-on sessions guide you through selecting natural ingredients, blending fragrances, and crafting your own unique candles. Whether you're a beginner or an enthusiast, our expert instructors will help you create beautiful, personalized candles that reflect your style. Perfect for a creative outing or a special gift, our workshops offer a relaxing and inspiring environment. Book your spot today and ignite your creativity with Noche!

Required Items

  • Soy Wax
  • Candle Wicks
  • Fragrance Oils
  • Dye (optional)
  • Double Boiler or Melting Pot
  • Thermometer
  • Pouring Pitcher
  • Mixing Utensils
  • Candle Molds or Containers
  • Adhesive (for securing wicks)
  • Protective Coverings (newspaper, gloves, apron)
